Helpful Habits

Maintain Order!
  • Make sure you have a clear schedule on paper.
  • Detect your main distracting elements.
  • Group your tasks into blocks.
  • Divide your day into several goals.
  • Set clear limitations.
People with the highest and best performance in the professional context know how to manage the time dedicated to working.
Pomodoro Technique
‚úåÔ∏èTake note
  • Split up the day into the tasks you are aiming to accomplish.
  • Sort this list in order of high priority to low priority.
  • Set your timer.
  • Take 5 minute rest breaks.
This technique is a way to manage your time, plan and execute tasks for increased productivity.
Food Tips
  • Eat fruits, vegetables and whole grains.
  • Avoid unhealthy fats in your food.
  • Consume Omega-3 fatty acids.
  • Educate yourself about the risks and benefits of taking vitamins or supplements.
  • Watch your weight.
In simple terms, eating unhealthy foods, such as processed foods or fast food, is very toxic to our body.
Stay Active
ü¶æSome Recommendations
  • Cardio and high intensity interval training (HIIT)
  • Tai chi for stress.
  • Boxing to burn the adrenaline.
  • Yoga to focus your mind.
  • Swimming de-stresses and focuses you.
Whether moderate or intense, exercising consistently offers mood-boosting benefits.
Work Stresses
  • Set Limits. Don't push yourself until you burn out.
  • Always reach out for help when you need it.
  • Don't make work the center of your life.
  • Put your health first.
Burnout, called professional burnout syndrome, is the response given by a person with strong physical and emotional exhaustion.
